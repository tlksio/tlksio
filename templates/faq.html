Frequently Asked Questions

tlks.io is the perfect place to showcase, discover, and discuss the best technical talks by the most influential speakers on the internet.

What is talks.io?

Developers, and enthusiasts about technology gather every day on tlks.io to share and discuss the best technical talks on the internet that helps them to learn and improve their skills.

How does it work?

Whenever you find a great talk or keynote that you enjoyed watching (including your own talks), you're more than welcome to submit it on tlks.io.

Which languages are supported on the talks?

By default, all talks we have published are in english. However, if you want to publish a talk with a different language, we recomend you to use a tag which specified the language the talk is spoken.
