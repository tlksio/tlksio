About tlks.io

Developers! Developers! Developers!

We love technical talks isn't it? We learn with them, we like to speak at them, and we really love to meet new people there. People like us, our crowd.

But there is a problem, every year more and more conferences are celebrated with a lot of great talks and speakers, and we can't attend to all of them ofcourse. And that's the reason we have built tlks.io! A place where you can publish, recomend and discover the latest and more popular talks driven by a community of developers and enthusiasts that love technical talks and enjoy watching them to learn about technology, about internet and about anything you can imagine.

We’re hoping to make it a little bit easier for the world to find out about cool talks.
